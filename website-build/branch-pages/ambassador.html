Become a VMF Coin Ambassador

Join Our Mission

As a VMF Coin Ambassador, you'll play a crucial role in spreading awareness about our mission to support veterans and military families. You'll be part of a community dedicated to making a real difference in the lives of those who have served.

Ambassador Benefits

As an ambassador, you'll receive:

  • Exclusive VMF Coin rewards
  • Early access to new features
  • Special recognition on our platform
  • Networking opportunities with other ambassadors
  • Direct input on platform development

How to Apply

To become an ambassador:

  1. Fill out the application form below
  2. Share your passion for supporting veterans
  3. Describe your experience with cryptocurrency
  4. Explain how you'll help spread the word about VMF Coin

In Memoriam

Donald A. Rose

Sgt. USMC
Served from 1949-1953 in World War 2 and was deployed to Japan. He was captured as a POW and was released after 6 months of containment. He loved his country and never forgot the others that weren't released. Donald passed away as a Proud Veteran in August of 1996.
Donald A. Rose

Donald J. Gray

PO3 USN
Served from 1955-1959 aboard the USS LeRay Wilson, Destroyer Escort as a Radio Man. Served with honor and pride, and was laid to rest 94 miles offshore of Oahu on August 15th 2020.
Donald J. Gray
