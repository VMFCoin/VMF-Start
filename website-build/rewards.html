How to Earn Rewards

VMF Coin offers rewards in three simple ways:
Hold VMF Coin
The longer you hold VMF Coin, the more rewards you can earn. Our system tracks your holding period and automatically calculates your rewards.
Participate in Community
Get involved in our community events, discussions, and voting to earn additional rewards. Your engagement helps shape the future of VMF Coin.
Refer New Members
When you refer new members to VMF Coin, you earn special rewards. The more members you refer, the more rewards you can earn. It's our way of thanking you for helping grow our community and support veterans.

Reward Tiers and Benefits

VMF Coin offers different reward tiers based on your level of participation and holding amount. Each tier comes with its own set of benefits:

Bronze Tier: Basic rewards and community access
Silver Tier: Enhanced rewards, voting rights, and exclusive content
Gold Tier: Premium rewards, priority support, and special event access
Platinum Tier: Maximum rewards, VIP status, and direct team communication

The more you participate and hold, the higher your tier and the better your rewards become.

Claiming Your Rewards

To claim your VMF Coin rewards, simply log into your account and visit the rewards dashboard. Your available rewards will be displayed, and you can choose how to use them. Some rewards can be automatically applied, while others may require manual claiming. We make the process simple and transparent so you can easily track and manage your rewards.

In Memoriam

Donald A. Rose

Sgt. USMC
Served from 1949-1953 in World War 2 and was deployed to Japan. He was captured as a POW and was released after 6 months of containment. He loved his country and never forgot the others that weren't released. Donald passed away as a Proud Veteran in August of 1996.
Donald A. Rose

Donald J. Gray

PO3 USN
Served from 1955-1959 aboard the USS LeRay Wilson, Destroyer Escort as a Radio Man. Served with honor and pride, and was laid to rest 94 miles offshore of Oahu on August 15th 2020.
Donald J. Gray
