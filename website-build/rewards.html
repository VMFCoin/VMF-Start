How to Earn Rewards

VMF Coin offers a comprehensive rewards system with multiple ways to earn tokens. The staking program provides 5% APR every 30 days for those who stake their coins, while top holders receive random token distributions every 90 days through surprise airdrops. These top holders also get exclusive direct communication with the core team, receiving early insights into upcoming developments and strategic updates before public announcements. Community participation is rewarded in several ways. Voters receive VMF tokens as a thank you for participating in community votes, and those who successfully refer charitable organizations to partner with VMF Coin receive bonus tokens. Community leaders who help build and strengthen the veteran-focused ecosystem are also recognized with rewards. Special programs include rewards for purchases, where every buy gives access to a guaranteed-win game with VMF token prizes. Top holders get exclusive access to limited-edition merchandise and NFT giveaways, along with priority access to new reward programs and special events. The community also recognizes and rewards social media engagement and community building efforts, providing opportunities for NFT rewards and special event access.

Claiming Your Rewards

VMF Coin rewards can be claimed through several straightforward processes. For staking rewards, the system automatically distributes 5% APR every 30 days to your wallet, requiring no manual action. Similarly, random 90-day airdrops are automatically sent to top holders' wallets without any claiming process needed.

For voting rewards, simply participate in community votes through the blockchain-based voting system, and the VMF tokens will be automatically distributed to your wallet. When referring charitable organizations, contact the VMF team at ambassadors@vmfcoin.com to process your referral bonus.

For purchase-related rewards, including the guaranteed-win game, complete your VMF Coin purchase and access the game through the provided link. Then play the game to earn your VMF token prize.

For VIP tier benefits, including early updates and team communication, top holders are automatically enrolled in the exclusive communication channel.

For community leadership rewards and special programs, including merchandise and NFTs, follow the instructions provided by the VMF team through official channels.
