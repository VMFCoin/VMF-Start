About VMF Coin

Our Mission

VMF Coin is dedicated to supporting veterans and military families through innovative digital solutions. Our mission is to create a sustainable ecosystem where every transaction contributes to the well-being of those who have served our nation.

We believe in transparency, community-driven decisions, and direct support to veteran-focused charities. Through our platform, we're building a bridge between digital innovation and traditional support systems for our military community.

Join us in making a difference in the lives of those who have given so much for our country.

Our platform is built on three core principles:

  • Transparency in all operations and transactions
  • Community-driven decision making
  • Direct support for veteran charities

When you invest in VMF Coin, you're not just buying a cryptocurrency - you're joining a movement dedicated to supporting those who have served our country. Every transaction contributes to our community pool, which is distributed to veteran charities on important military observance days.

Our team is comprised of veterans, military family members, and cryptocurrency experts who are passionate about making a difference. We're committed to building a platform that not only provides value to investors but also creates lasting positive impact for our veteran community.

In Memoriam

Donald A. Rose

Sgt. USMC
Served from 1949-1953 in World War 2 and was deployed to Japan. He was captured as a POW and was released after 6 months of containment. He loved his country and never forgot the others that weren't released. Donald passed away as a Proud Veteran in August of 1996.
Donald A. Rose

Donald J. Gray

PO3 USN
Served from 1955-1959 aboard the USS LeRay Wilson, Destroyer Escort as a Radio Man. Served with honor and pride, and was laid to rest 94 miles offshore of Oahu on August 15th 2020.
Donald J. Gray
