Become An Ambassador

Community Leadership

Join VMF Coin's leadership team as a Community Leader - we welcome veterans, military families, crypto enthusiasts, and supporters who want to build something amazing together! Just reach out to us at Ambassadors to become part of our dynamic team shaping the future of blockchain and veteran communities.

Marketing Support

VMF Ambassadors help spread awareness about our platform and its mission to support veteran charities through social media and community outreach. They build relationships with potential partner organizations and help veteran charities discover VMF's benefits.

Exclusive Benefits

As a VMF Ambassador, you'll earn VIP status and VMF coins for championing our mission! You'll also get early access to programs, partner perks, and the chance to shape our community's future while building bridges between veterans and crypto innovators.

Help us revolutionize veteran support

As an ambassador, you'll help connect veteran charities with our innovative blockchain platform and make a real difference in the lives of those who served. You'll get to represent VMF Coin at veteran-focused events, learn about cutting-edge blockchain technology, and build relationships with veteran organizations. We're looking for people who are passionate about supporting veterans and have strong communication skills. You'll get a special ambassador badge for your social media, priority access to new features, and opportunities to contribute to community governance.   • Exclusive VMF Coin merchandise (hoodies, hats, and more!)
  • Early access to new platform features
  • Special recognition in our community channels

  • Platform governance voting rights
