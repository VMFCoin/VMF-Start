What VMF Coin Does for Veteran Charities

VMF Coin is a new way to help veterans and military families through digital technology. Here's how we help charities:

We make it easy for charities to get the money they need right away, without any middlemen taking a cut. Our system is like a digital donation box that's transparent and fair.

Every time someone uses our system, a small amount goes into a special fund for charities. The community decides which charities get the money, and we give it out seven times a year on important military holidays like Memorial Day and Veterans Day.

This way, charities can focus on helping veterans instead of dealing with paperwork and waiting for money. We make sure everything is tracked and transparent, so everyone knows their donations are making a difference.
